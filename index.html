001
AI has undoubtedly changed art and its perception.

Tools and machinery of their time have always influenced art. AI asks an increasingly broader audience a familiar and difficult question that has eluded resolution through time.

"What is art?"

Truly. What is art? A great question positioned to be resolved no sooner the answer to "What is a soul?" or "What is the meaning of life?"

Digital tooling in music, film, photography has been slowly embraced, not without conflict, with delay, not without disagreement, and not without holdouts. Analog methods maintain a degree of intimacy with imprecision, and that very imprecision is at the heart of what makes art, art.

AI has reaffirmed a personal definition for art. One that may not be agreed by all, but a definition that begins to loosely cup its hands around the liquid being that is art, capturing some but by no means a true vessel.

Precision through imprecision is art. That noisy translation of expression and communication where artist to viewer message or intention is not always clear. That signal degradation is an essence of art that the realm of digital has eroded with its exactness.

This volume explores my process and experience creating a means to translate digital art into the physical world through the use of machine and redigitizing it for display on a screen once more.

It is: Sloppy. Imperfect. Faulty. Wasteful. Time consuming. Laborious. Inconsistent.

And beautiful.

This is art.

This is wryco.
