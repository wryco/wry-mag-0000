Hello!

I'm lewis.

Welcome to the 0th edition of the wryco art zine.

The following is a selection of my art, digitally curated and edited into a digital zine. In this work, I explore the tension between an imagined future and its nostalgia for our present. I use digital, software, and 3d printers weilding pens to create art.

One weekend in September 2025, I gave my 3d printer a pen, and since then, I've been slowly figuring out how to make art with it. Some of the art in this collection was made using it, and you'll be introduced to the printers shortly.

Software has traditionally been my outlet and modality to create. I'm not a traditional artist. My basis in art is web design. Most of the layout and editing below is actually HTML and CSS, so be sure to ctrl + shift + i to check out the code!

Enjoy.

Visit https://wryco.com/art for more my complete portfolio and contact details for commissions and inquiries.
